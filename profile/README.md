Welcome to the ***Wireless Intelligent Networks for FutureG with Security (WINGS) Lab*** based at [University of Hawaii at Mānoa](https://ece.hawaii.edu/home/) and led by [Dr. Xiaochan Xue](https://luna-xue.github.io/). Our team, comprising PhD researchers, graduate students, and collaborators, and works at the intersection of physical-layer security, ISAC sensing, and edge intelligence to build secure, resilient, and deployable AI-RAN/O-RAN platforms.


## 🔬 Core Research Directions
- Develop **trustworthy AI-native wireless systems** integrating communication, sensing, and intelligence  
- Bridge **physical-layer security**, **ISAC (Integrated Sensing and Communication)**, and **edge learning at the RAN**  
- Design and prototype **privacy-preserving and resilient AI-RAN / O-RAN architectures**  
- Build **end-to-end experimental testbeds** for real-world validation of next-generation wireless systems  
- Investigate **security against adaptive and generative AI-driven attacks** in wireless networks  
- Enable **robust and secure operation of AI-native networks under adversarial conditions**  
- Explore **intent-aware network control using Theory-of-Mind (ToM)-inspired agents**  
- Advance **multi-stakeholder decision-making frameworks** for future AI-RAN ecosystems  
- Focus on **practical, deployable solutions bridging theory, systems, and experimentation**
